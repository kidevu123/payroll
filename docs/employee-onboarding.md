# Employee onboarding

Welcome. This is your guide to the payroll app.

## Sign in for the first time

Your manager added you. They'll either give you a temporary password or invite you to set one. Open the link they sent on your phone.

Tip: install the app to your home screen. On iOS Safari, tap the share button → **Add to Home Screen**. On Android Chrome, tap the menu → **Install app**.

## The four tabs

**Home** — your week so far. Hours, projected pay, days remaining. Any missed-punch alerts show up here with a **Fix this** link. Quick-action buttons for fixing a punch or requesting time off.

**Time** — last five weeks of your punches grouped by week. Tap a day to see in/out times and an "edited" indicator if your manager fixed something.

**Pay** — your payslips, newest first. Three states:
- **Pending** — payroll hasn't published yet.
- **Published** — your payslip is ready. Tap to open the PDF.
- **Acknowledged** — you've opened it and confirmed.

When you tap a published payslip, you see the PDF inline plus a button **"I have reviewed this payslip"**. That's all that's needed — no signing, no extra steps.

**Profile** — display name, phone, language. Sensitive fields (legal name, email) are read-only; if you need to change those, ask your manager. Sign out is at the bottom.

## Notifications

Open **Profile → Notifications** and tap **Enable push notifications**. Your phone will ask permission. Once granted, you get a notification when:

- A missed-punch alert needs your fix.
- Payroll publishes your latest payslip.
- A request you submitted (fix-punch or time off) is resolved.

To turn it off, hit **Disable** on the same screen.

## What if something's wrong with my hours?

If you forgot to clock in or out, you'll see a **NO_PUNCH** or **MISSING_OUT** alert on Home. Tap it, fill in what your real clock times were, write a quick note (e.g. "forgot to badge in, was here from 8 to 4"), submit. Your manager gets pinged and reviews.

If you don't see an alert but your hours look wrong: open Time, find the day, screenshot it, send to your manager. The fix-punch flow is for missing data, not disagreements.

## Time off

Hit **Request time off** from Home. Pick start + end dates, type (Personal / Sick / Unpaid / Other), optional reason. Your manager approves or rejects. Approved time-off suppresses the **NO_PUNCH** alerts that would otherwise fire on those days.

## Spanish

Set your language to Español on the Profile screen. The app reloads in Spanish on your next page load.

## Privacy

Your data lives on a server your employer runs. The app caches an offline shell on your device but never caches your hours, pay, or alerts — those re-fetch every time you open the app.
